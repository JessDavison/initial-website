Why We’re Here

The Policy Lab is a new partnership between the State of Rhode Island and Brown University, where experts from government, universities, and the community collaborate on research tailored to inform decisions about how to improve policy and program operations.

The partnership is also a vehicle for increasing staff training and capacity to complete such work, by way of onsite training and courses, fellowships, speaker and event series, curating a statewide integrated data system, and other activities to foster a community of practice around the research.

Theory of Change

We look to improve policy outcomes through three focus areas:

Generating evidence to fill knowledge gaps

Integrating that evidence into planning, budget, procurement, and performance management processes

Building coalitions across government, academic institutions, and community organizations to support evidence-based policy

The three areas are extremely codependent.

While many approaches to this work focus on one or two areas at a time, building a culture of evidence-based policymaking requires us to pursue all three simultaneously.
